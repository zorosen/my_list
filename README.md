📝 My List
A lightweight and efficient application to manage, track, and organize your personal lists.

Create & Edit: Easily add new items or modify existing ones.

Categories: Organize items by custom tags or categories.

Search & Filter: Quickly find exactly what you're looking for.

Persistent Storage: Your data is saved locally or synced to a database.

Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile.
